Festival of Lights: Diwali, also known as Deepavali, is celebrated as the Festival of Lights, symbolizing the victory of light over darkness and good over evil. Religious Significance: It holds significance for Hindus, Jains, Sikhs, and some Buddhists. For Hindus, it marks the return of Lord Rama to Ayodhya after defeating Ravana.
